# Cyber Threat Intelligence Analysis

## Introduction
Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) is a crucial part of modern cybersecurity strategies. It involves the collection and analysis of information about potential or current attacks that threaten an organization’s assets.

## Objectives
- To understand the threat landscape.
- To gather and analyze threat data.
- To support decision-making and response actions.

## Components of Cyber Threat Intelligence
1. **Threat Data Collection:** Gathering raw data from various sources.
2. **Analysis:** Interpreting the collected data to find patterns and indicators of compromise (IOCs).
3. **Dissemination:** Sharing the intelligence with the relevant stakeholders.

## Conclusion
Effective CTI enables organizations to be proactive rather than reactive in their cybersecurity efforts.
